Concept & Theme

A pyromaniac/inventor macaque monkey. Always tinkering with explosives — bombs, dynamite, aerosols. Wears overalls stained with gunpowder and hole-ridden work gloves.

Combat Archetype

Hybrid Zoner / Melee Rushdown. Controls space with bounce bombs, sets defensive mines to cover landings, and commits with high-launch combos.
