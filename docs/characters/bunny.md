## Concept

A white-furred rabbit in ninja-esque martial arts attire. A deadly bunny who fights with powerful kicks and a giant carrot as a weapon. What appears cute is actually a fierce close-quarters combatant ‚Äî fast, technical, and hit-and-run.

The character's "silliness" comes from the **carrot** (weapon, projectile, slam) rather than being a joke character. Think **Overgrowth √ó Lee Sin** ‚Äî serious martial arts with slight vegetable-themed absurdity.

## Archetype

**Agile melee assassin / kick-focused brawler** ‚Äî rushdown with mobility tools.
- LMB = 3-hit kick combo (low ‚Üí medium ‚Üí high)
- RMB = carrot slam (ground AoE)
- Q = carrot boomerang (poke + mark setup for R, mark lasts ~5s)
- E = backflip kick (disengage / mobility)
- R = dragon kick (powerful finisher, boosted if target marked by Q)
- F = Tempest of the Hare (ult ‚Äî spin in place, cyclone pulls enemies toward center, final launcher kick)

## Unity Pipeline Notes

- Model FBX at `globalScale: 1` ‚Äî same as Manki
- All 14 animation FBXs had clips renamed from `mixamo.com` to their filenames via `ModelImporter.clipAnimations`
- Animator generated via right-click `Assets/Art/Characters/bunny/` ‚Üí `Create SlopArena Animator`
- The generator uses a **two-pass save/reload** to avoid Unity Editor NRE on AnyState transitions (pass 1: create states + regular transitions ‚Üí save ‚Üí reload ‚Üí pass 2: add AnyState transitions)
- `applyRootMotion = false` on the prefab Animator ‚Äî server-authoritative position
