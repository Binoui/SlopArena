## System Mechanics

### Talents / God Pass
Each god had a **God Pass** (1-20) unlocking traits, blessings, and skins:
- **Divine Traits:** Core passive modifiers (3 per god: default + GP7 + GP16)
- **Minor Traits:** Smaller stat upgrades
- **Lesser Blessings:** Minor situational buffs
- **Greater Blessings:** Major effects (e.g. ult charge on KO, ability reset on ult)

### Universal Moves
All gods share:
- **Dodge** (Shift): Short dash, 3-4s cooldown, invincibility frames
- **Light Attack** (LMB): 2-4 hit combo depending on god
- **Heavy Attack** (RMB): Tap or hold for charge variants
- **Air variants** (in air + LMB/RMB): Every god has unique air attacks

### Vulnerability System
- Each hit increases the target's **Vulnerability** percentage
- Knockback scales with Vulnerability — higher vuln = further knockback
- Ring-out kills happen when knockback pushes enemies past arena boundaries
- Thanatos interacts uniquely: heals Vulnerability, deals bonus dmg to targets >100%

### Combat Style
- Damage numbers are small (3-30 range). Kills come from **ring-out**, not HP depletion
- Every ability has clear **counterplay** (telegraphed, dodgeable)
- No passive innates — all power comes from abilities + chosen talents

## Design Lessons for SlopArena

1. **Small kits work:** 4 abilities + 2 attacks = room for depth without complexity
2. **Every god has clear identity:** No overlap in role/function
3. **Ring-out > HP war:** Makes positioning matter more than stat checking
4. **Self-damage (Thanatos) is interesting risk/reward**
5. **Zone control (Ymir wall, Thor wall) creates strategic depth**
6. **Clone/swap (Loki, Sun Wukong) adds outplay potential**
7. **Talents modify playstyle without class changes**
8. **Air attacks matter:** Every god has distinct air LMB/RMB — adds vertical depth
9. **Hold heavy variants** give every god a charge option on basic attacks
10. **4-hit combos (Amaterasu, Izanami)** feel different from 3-hit — pacing variety
